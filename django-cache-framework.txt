
# why caching:
You can cache a single query, the output of a specific view, parts of rendered template content, or your entire site
This translates into shorter response times and less processing on the server side.

- backends.memcached.MemcachedCache
- backends.db.DatabaseCache
- backends.filebased.FileBasedCache
- backends.locmem.LocMemCache
- backends.dummy.DummyCache
